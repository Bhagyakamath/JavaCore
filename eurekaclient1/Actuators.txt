3)Commonly Used Endpoints

/health: Displays the application's health status.

/info: Provides general application information.

/metrics: Shows application metrics, including JVM and custom metrics.

/loggers: Allows querying and modifying logging levels.

/beans: Lists all Spring beans in the application.

/threaddump: Performs a thread dump of the JVM.

/scheduledtasks: Displays details about scheduled tasks.

/prometheus: Exposes metrics in a format compatible with Prometheus.
